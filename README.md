Welcome to Satlex Community
Satlex is a decentralized, AI-powered knowledge network. There are no admins, no passwords, and no central authority. You learn by explaining complex topics, earning Satlex Coins (SC), and trading knowledge with peers.
🔐 1. Your Digital Identity (No Passwords)
 * The Seed Phrase: When you join, you generate a 12-word "seed phrase." This is your unique, cryptographically secure identity.
 * Keep It Safe: Write these 12 words down offline. There is no email login and no "forgot password" button. If you lose your words, you lose your account.
 * Room ID: Your seed phrase generates your public "Room ID," which acts as your username and address on the network.
🧠 2. The Feynman AI Challenge (Learn & Earn)
 * Explain to Learn: Choose a daily challenge or enter a custom topic (Math, Physics, AI, etc.) and explain it as if teaching a 10-year-old, a peer, or an expert.
 * Multiple Formats: Submit your explanation via text, upload an audio recording, or snap a picture of your handwritten notes.
 * AI Grading: The Groq-powered AI evaluates your explanation using the "Feynman Technique," grading you out of 10 and providing detailed feedback.
 * Earn SC & XP: Score an 8/10 or higher to prove your knowledge and automatically earn Satlex Coins (SC) and Experience Points (XP).
🎮 3. Ranks, Streaks & Gamification
 * Level Up: Accumulate XP to climb the ranks from Novice to Sage.
 * Daily Streaks: Completing an explanation every day builds your streak, which multiplies your XP earnings.
 * Streak Freezes: If you need to miss a day, use your SC to buy a "Streak Freeze" from the Home page to protect your progress.
 * Leaderboards: Compete with friends and the global community on the weekly XP leaderboard.
🛒 4. The Knowledge Marketplace
 * Buy Materials: Spend your earned SC on premium study notes, deep-dive guides, and resources created by other users.
 * Sell Your Work: Upload your own notes or courses and set a price. You keep a percentage of the SC from every sale.
 * Community Moderation: High-reputation "Genuine Users" can flag low-quality content. If an item gets 10 flags, it is automatically removed, and all buyers are fully refunded.
❓ 5. The Doubt Arena
 * Ask Questions: Post your toughest academic questions and assign a subject.
 * Provide Answers: Help peers by answering their open doubts.
 * Rate & Tip: The person who asked the question can rate your answer (1 to 5 stars) and send you an SC tip. Earning 5-star ratings boosts your account's reputation.
🤝 6. Social & Collaboration
 * Direct Messages: Add friends, follow top creators, and chat securely. You can also send peer-to-peer SC tips directly inside your DMs.
 * P2P Video Calling: Hop on a direct, peer-to-peer video stream to study together in real-time by entering a friend's Room ID.
 * Public Profiles: View other members' XP, current streaks, total AI evaluations, and average scores.
